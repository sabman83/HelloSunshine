Simply Delicious

Kitchen App

Enjoy, learn and love cooking. Adopt a Healthier Lifestyle. Large range of mouth watering recipes. From Desserts to authentic dishes, Quick recipes for busy people, Chef special recipes which will challenge the cooking skills.

my role

I wanted to create a responsive web app specifically targeting people who want to adopt a healthier lifestyle. To design a responsive web app for finding recipes that meet the need of the users and solves the problems they are facing with existing apps.

research

I started this process by conducting market research on existing apps and websites. I conducted user interviews and gathered information about customers and competitors.

that's where Simply Delicious App comes in.

There are many apps, websites and cooking books but still people are having problem following them, they want simple steps and brand new recipes.

get inspired

I started my design with low fidelity wireframes. I studied different apps and made my design simpler to reach to every kind of user.
