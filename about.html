About Me

I’m Minal ! I’m a UI and Visual Designer with a background in Computer Engineering. My past work fostered not only a passion for sustainability, but also for communicating strong messaging in an accessible way, which led me to user interface design. I’ve worked with large companies, startups, and non-profit organizations, and the thread that connects them all is a strong driving mission. As a hobby illustrator and graphic designer, I have an eye for good design and brand development, while my engineering background has given me a knack for creative problem solving, fast learning, and analytical thinking. I’m looking forward to applying those skills to create appealing and accessible user-centric designs.

I am constantly trying new things and looking into new creative avenues. Lasting education and discovery is a pattern I strive towards in my daily life and my career. I am driven by passion and interest, which is why I choose to work on things I believe in, like sustainability and public health.

When I'm not designing, you can find me taking a dance class, scrolling through Facebook marketplace for home decor, or trying to keep my 50 (ish) houseplants alive.
